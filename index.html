Note: There are three conditions for a coin to be highlighted (make alert sound).
  1. If volume change in the interval is greater then or equal to "Min vol change to highlight"
  2. If price change in the interval is greater then or equal to "Min price change to highlight"
  3. If volume in the interval is greater compared to volume in previous interval AND coin listed in previous interval
If either of the three is true, the coin will be highlighted, you can hover over the highlighted coin to know which condition is true.
